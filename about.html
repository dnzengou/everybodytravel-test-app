Hey :)

Alexandre Bodart Pinto, founder of Everybody Travel had a motorbike accident at the age of 16 and became quadriplegic. After more than 1 year in rehabilitation he decided to continue to practice his passion: Traveling around the world…

He realized during his travels that many people have to face with mobility problems and that traveling when you are a PRM (person with reduced mobility) can easily turn into a nightmare if you don’t have access to the right information. Organizing his trips took him so much time that he decided he has to do something to facilitate the life of all those who have problems of mobility, by centralizing all the information in one place, and by creating the largest community of persons with reduced mobility. And this is how

Everybody Travel is born….
